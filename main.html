Upcoming Special Events:

Super Bowl:

Come to the Game Day Grill the week of the Super Bowl and show your football enthusiasm with our limited time Super Bowl burger meal! Try our mouthwatering 1/2 pound burger crafted on a brioche bun, layered with juicy tomatoes, crisp red onions, roasted peppers, rich cheddar cheese, fresh romaine lettuce, pepperoni, and crowned with our signature bacon-jam special sauce.

Trivia Night:
Join us every Friday for a fun night of trivia! Play with your friends in casual trivia games, and test your sports knowledge throughout the evening. Starts at 6pm and ends at closing.

Reservation Info:

Reservations are not required, but reccomended if you have a large group, and on busy nights, such as Fridays and weekends, especially during the summer. To schedule a reservation, call the number (906)-204-9970 and create a reservation.
