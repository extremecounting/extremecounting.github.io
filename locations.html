Locations:

Game Day Grill is proud to serve food in 3 locations: Escanaba, MI; Grand Rapids, MI; and Chicago, IL.
Escanaba was our first location, and has been serving local customers for over a decade.
Grand Rapids is our most popular location, serving almost 1000 guests on average per day.
Chicago is our newest location, but is quickly becoming a big hit among locals!
We hope to further expand across the nation, bringing Game Day Grill to everyone!
